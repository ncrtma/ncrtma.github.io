Submission Guidelines

Authors willing to present their research work can submit the abstract electronically (not more than 300 words) to ncrtma@gmail.com

or before29/01/2018.

All submitted papers will be reviewed by Advisory Committee of the conference and a proceeding with all accepted papers will be released on the first day of the conference.

The scrutinized and accepted papers will be published in:

  • International Journal of Pure And Applied Mathematics-IJPAM(Scopus Indexed) at a concessional cost of Rs. 3000/- only.

  • South East Journal of Mathematics and Mathematical Sciences(UGC Approved) at concessional cost of Rs. 300/- per page.
