The National Conference on Recent Trends in Mathematics & its Applications (NCRTMA'16) is being organized by the Department of Mathematics, SRM University (city campus), Vadapalani, Chennai. This conference is the premier forum for the presentation of new advances, research results and to discuss the future trends in the field of mathematics
